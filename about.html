WE ARE JESUS GOSPEL COMMUNITY CHURCH!!!

The Bible is filled with messages of encouragement, offering hope and strength to those who feel weary or discouraged. One of the most powerful reminders comes from Isaiah 41:10, which says, "So do not fear, for I am with you; do not be dismayed, for I am your God. I will strengthen you and help you; I will uphold you with my righteous right hand." This verse reassures us that no matter what challenges we face, God is always present, ready to guide and support us. His love is steadfast, and His promises are unchanging. When we feel overwhelmed, we can find comfort in knowing that God’s strength is made perfect in our weakness. He invites us to cast our burdens upon Him, assuring us that He cares deeply for us. No matter the circumstances, we can hold on to the truth that God is with us, giving us courage and the strength to overcome.

Our Purpose.

At Jesus Gospel Community Church (JGCC), our purpose is to bring people into a deeper relationship with God by worshiping Him in truth and spirit. We strive to be a community where individuals can experience the transformative power of God’s love, grace, and truth. Our goal is to cultivate a space where every person, regardless of background, can encounter God, grow in their faith, and live out their purpose. We believe in creating an environment that encourages spiritual growth, where people can find healing, hope, and a sense of belonging. Through prayer, teaching, and fellowship, we aim to build a vibrant community centered around Christ, one that reflects His love and light to the world.

Our Mission.

Our mission at JGCC is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with everyone and to make disciples who are committed to living out God’s Word. We are dedicated to serving our community, uplifting those in need, and spreading a message of hope, peace, and salvation.

God is spirit [the Source of life, yet invisible to mankind], and those who worship Him must worship in spirit and truth.

John 4:24 AMP

Our Values.

Identity in Christ

Our identity in Christ is the foundation of who we are. The Bible tells us that when we accept Christ, we become a new creation; the old has gone, and the new has come (2 Corinthians 5:17). This means that our worth is no longer defined by the world but by God’s love and grace. In Christ, we are loved, forgiven, and chosen. We are called children of God, and nothing can separate us from His love. Our identity is secure, not because of what we have done, but because of who Christ is and what He has done for us.

Church Is Family

We believe that the church is not just a place of worship but a family united in Christ. In this sacred community, we find support, encouragement, and accountability, mirroring the love and unity that Jesus prayed for among His followers (John 17:21). As members of this family, we are called to be devoted to one another in love (Romans 12:10), fostering an environment where every individual feels valued and cherished. Together, we celebrate victories, navigate challenges, and share life’s journey, embodying the essence of true fellowship as we grow in our relationship with God and each other.

Kingdom Culture

At Jesus Gospel Community Church, we embrace the values of Kingdom culture, striving to reflect God's love and righteousness in all aspects of our lives. This culture is characterized by grace, compassion, and service, as we are called to love one another just as Christ loved us (John 13:34). We actively seek to embody the principles of the Kingdom, living out our faith through acts of kindness and generosity, and encouraging one another to grow in spiritual maturity (Ephesians 4:15-16). Together, we aim to be a light in our community, shining brightly and drawing others to the transformative power of God's love (Matthew 5:14-16).

Spirit Led Life

At Jesus Gospel Community Church, we are committed to living a Spirit-led life, guided by the Holy Spirit in our daily decisions and interactions. This transformative journey empowers us to reflect Christ's character, as we are reminded in Galatians 5:22-23 of the fruits of the Spirit: love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. By yielding to the Spirit, we cultivate a deeper relationship with God and one another, allowing His guidance to shape our thoughts, actions, and priorities. Together, we seek to walk in the Spirit, bringing hope and healing to our community as we live out our faith authentically and boldly.


What We Believe.

The Authority Of Scripture.

We believe that the Bible is the inspired and infallible Word of God, serving as the ultimate authority for faith and practice (2 Timothy 3:16-17). It reveals God’s character, His plan for humanity, and the pathway to salvation through Jesus Christ. As we study and apply Scripture in our lives, we grow in wisdom and understanding, equipping ourselves to fulfill our God-given purpose.

The Trinity.

We believe in one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit (Matthew 28:19). Each person of the Trinity is fully God, co-equal and co-eternal, and together they work in perfect unity to accomplish God's redemptive plan. This profound mystery invites us into a relationship with God, as we experience His love, grace, and guidance through the work of the Father, the sacrifice of the Son, and the empowerment of the Holy Spirit.

Salvation Through Christ

We believe that salvation is a gift from God, available to all who place their faith in Jesus Christ as their Lord and Savior (John 3:16). Through His death and resurrection, Jesus provided the means for our reconciliation with God, offering forgiveness for our sins and the promise of eternal life. This transformative experience changes our hearts and empowers us to live in accordance with His will.

The Great Commision.

We believe that every believer is called to fulfill the Great Commission by sharing the Gospel and making disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19-20). This mission reflects the heart of God for humanity and drives our commitment to outreach and evangelism. As we engage our community and beyond, we seek to embody Christ’s love and truth, inviting others to experience the hope and transformation found in Him.
